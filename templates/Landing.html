Welcome to YouTube To Spotify Converter!

    Follow these steps below to convert your Youtube playlist to a Spotify Playlist.

  • 1. Connect your Spotify account. - So as to automatically create a playlist
  • 2. Connect your YouTube account. This connects to your youtube account to get your private and public playlists.
  • 3. Enter the link of the playlist you want to convert or select from the list of your playlists.
  • 4. Press 'Convert to Spotify Playlist.'
  • 5. Go to your Spotify Library and enjoy your Music.
